    CARGO INSURANCE

    Cargo insurance is a type of insurance that provides coverage against loss or damage to goods during transportation. This insurance is crucial for businesses that ship products internationally or domestically, as it offers protection against a wide range of risks, including theft, natural disasters, accidents, and mishandling. By securing cargo insurance, businesses can ensure financial stability in case of unforeseen incidents that could otherwise lead to significant losses.
